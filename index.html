Services

What i offer

Analysis of Health Service Data

Data analysis techniques are used in the health sector to improvise various medical research needs. The method used is to collect and process data from clinical information centers, such as EHR, electronic records, patient health records, and public health records.

Health Service Facility Performance

The performance of health service facilities includes various aspects that contribute to the quality and effectiveness of services provided to patients. Several things have been done to improve the performance of health service facilities, including: Improving Accessibility, Providing Adequate Resources, Improving Service Quality.

Medical Record Filing System

The medical record filing system is the process of storing, organizing, and managing patient medical records in a health facility. The following are some of the things that are done in the medical record filing system: Making and Maintaining Medical Records, Organizing Medical Records, Security and Confidentiality, System Integration.

Disease Diagnosis

Identify and understand the medical condition of the patient. By making an accurate diagnosis, the medical recorder can provide important information for proper care and treatment.
